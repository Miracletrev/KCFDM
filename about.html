About KCDM

Learn more about our history, mission, and leadership.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ, to bring deliverance to the captive, and to build a community of believers rooted in faith, hope, and love. We are committed to serving God and our neighbors with compassion and integrity.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of light in our community, a place where the hurting can find healing, the lost can find their way home, and every individual can grow in their relationship with God.
